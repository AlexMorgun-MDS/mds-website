About

Database expertise, without the overhead.

Morgun Database Solutions LLC (MDS) is an independent consultancy focused on one thing: making data systems that are fast, dependable, and easy to build on. We partner with startups and established teams alike to design new databases, rescue struggling ones, and keep production systems healthy.

From schema design and query tuning to cloud migrations and day-to-day administration, we bring senior, hands-on experience across relational and modern data platforms — so you get the depth of a specialist without hiring a full-time DBA.
